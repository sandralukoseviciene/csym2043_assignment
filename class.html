Overview of 6 week remote working employee wellbeing programme

Each week will consist of short videos to watch (max ten mins) and related activities to complete around specific topics. Topics are aimed at supporting employee productivity by providing tools to boost wellbeing and promote positive behavioural changes.

Week One

Supporting your own wellbeing at home and getting started with your journey (video) Wellbeing and work activity Using online journal (once a week entry) One to one review (once a week meeting)

Week Two

The home working environment - Posture (video), separating work from home (video) Home working activity Online journal (once a week entry) One to one review (once a week meeting)

Week Three

Chair workout and increased mobility as your desk (video) Thinking about your diet and H20 activity Online journal (once a week entry) One to one review (once a week meeting)

Week Four

Yoga and breathing to enhance wellbeing (video) Dealing with difficult relations Get creative and inspired activity Online journal (once a week entry) One to one review (once a week review)

Week Five

Yoga and breathing to enhance wellbeing (video) Setting intentions and goals activity Free writing activity Online journal One to one review
